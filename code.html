All of my projects are hosted on GitHub.

The Journal Pump

Aimed to help students solve problems related to writing essays and schoolwork. Also features a thesis management system that automatically generates a template you can work on depending on your research title. It enables collaboration through the cloud.

Lost Treasures Index

Utilizes data science to help assess all the world's educational, scientific, and cultural treasures' survival rate and the individual impactful factors affecting it. It also indexes these treasures according to their survival rate through the next century.

The Red Socks Initiative

It is a warning system designed for Region 4A which utilizes Machine Learning, Satellite Surveillance Data, and Telemetry against natural disasters such as flash floods, earthquakes and typhoons.

Quantum Society of the Philippines Website

A mockup website that was assigned to me when I was applying for a Fellowship under OneQuantum Philippines, a national organization aiming to spread and culture technologies related to quantum computing in the Philippines.
